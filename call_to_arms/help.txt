```Apex Bot Commands```
**ta.redeem [Common/Novice/Specialist/Legendary]**: Draws a random item out of a specified lootbox.

**ta.contents [Common/Novice/Specialist/Legendary]**: Posts all of the items contained within a specified lootbox.

**ta.info [Item]**: Posts detailed information on the specified item.

**ta.steal**: Determines whether a loser keeps his/her equipped item.

**ta.help**: Posts the help screen.
